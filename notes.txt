Guide:
Memorization (Tells us how big the MLP needs to be)
    With chunk_size > sequence length, no ICL happens to the MLP, so short_conv, SWA, and the trainable init are all the learns.
    RoPE is crucial to prevent overfitting to the order of the sequence for long sequences. Conv hurts performance. Make the MLP
    as big as possible. I think lower heads is good.
Compression
    Big Dim trains slower but better over time. Follows a same trend as memorization (big mlps), stacking mlp-attentions is good but slow. 
    Conv+Rope is good. Conv no Rope is fine. Rope no Conv is fine.
ICR
    I think muon/momentum stabilizes the MLP updates when dim_inner is too big
Fuzzy ICR 
    Depth seems to matter. Conv helps a lot as a trick for a deeper model.
Selective Copying
    Requires rope, otherwise I think its an easy one? not sure how close to 99% though we will get.
